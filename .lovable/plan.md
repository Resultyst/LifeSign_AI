10. **Innovation Highlights** — No paid APIs, no LLMs, fully on-device AI, zero-cost architecture
11. **Accessibility** — WCAG 2.1 AA, large touch targets, screen reader support, high contrast
12. **Future Roadmap** — BSL support, EMR integration, native apps
